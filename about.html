About Matcha in Paris

Welcome to my hand-poured guide to the matcha corners of Paris. Every spot here has been sipped, savoured, and judged by me—no algorithms, no sponsorships, just honest cups. You’ll find tasting notes, candid photos, and live map links so you can follow the trail yourself.

Why this exists: When I went looking for Paris’s best matcha, I couldn’t find a single place that gathered them all with honest reviews. So I decided to make one.

Know a hidden gem? Share it with me: ellinakhrais@gmail.com.
